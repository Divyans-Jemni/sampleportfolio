Publication

"Prediction of Ground Water Level in Rajasthan State Using Machine Learning"

Procedia Computer Science, Vol 218, 2023, (pp 1702-1711), ISSN 1877-0509

• The purpose of the study was to score a model that can explicitly predict the fluctuation in the groundwater level based on various external factors that are used as features in this research
• The drastic groundwater levels in different parts of Rajasthan can be attributed to many factors, principally hydrogeological formation, Precipitation, and chemical composition
• All factors discussed in the paper are strongly correlated to the resulting fluctuation in the GWL
• For the accuracy and intricacy of the study, strongly/moderately correlated components were chosen
